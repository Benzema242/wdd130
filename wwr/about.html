History

Our rafting company was founded in 2020 with a passion for adventure and the great outdoors. Since then, we have been dedicated to providing unforgettable experiences on the water. Our experienced guides are committed to ensuring your safety and enjoyment on every trip. We are a family based business that values community and the environment. We believe in sustainable practices and strive to minimize our impact on the beautiful rivers we explore. Over the years, we have expanded our offerings to include a variety of trips suitable for all skill levels. From gentle floats to thrilling whitewater adventures, we have something for everyone. Our team is constantly exploring new routes and improving our services to provide you with the best possible experience. Join us as we continue to grow and share our love for rafting with adventurers from all walks of life. Whether you're looking for a peaceful day on the river or an adrenaline-pumping adventure, we are here to make your rafting dreams come true. Thank you for being a part of our journey!
