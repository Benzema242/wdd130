Bahamas

The Bahamas is a beautiful country made up of over 700 islands and cays. It is known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant culture. The Bahamas is a popular destination for tourists from around the world, offering a wide range of activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving, and beachcombing. The capital city, Nassau, is located on the island of New Providence and is home to many historic sites, vibrant markets, and lively nightlife.
